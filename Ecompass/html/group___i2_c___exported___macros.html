  • I2C_FLAG_DUALF: Dual flag
  • I2C_FLAG_GENCALL: General call header flag
  • I2C_FLAG_TRA: Transmitter/Receiver flag
  • I2C_FLAG_BUSY: Bus busy flag
  • I2C_FLAG_MSL: Master/Slave flag
